Tom Brady:

The Greatest Football Player Ever And The Only One With 7 Rings

Tom Brady's Tough Football Career:

Tom Brady is known as the greatest football player to ever play the game, but it was not an easy task for him. At Michigan, he was the 7th backup quarterback on the roster and he was given a full ride, but had to work daily with great consistency. After three years, Tom Brady was named the starter for Michigan. After that, he tried out for the NFL and was drafted by the Patriots, in which he was the Third-String Quarterback and after taking the opportunity and not letting it slip, he became the starter backup leading the Patriots to six super bowl rings.

Acolades:

  • 7-time Super Bowl champion
  • 5-time Super Bowl MVP
  • 3-time NFL MVP
  • 14-time Pro Bowl selection
  • 3-time First-team All-Pro
  • 2-time Second-team All-Pro
  • NFL Comeback Player of the Year
  • NFL Offensive Player of the Year
  • NFL Passing touchdowns leader
  • NFL passing yards leader
  • NFL 2010s All-Decade Team
  • NFL 100th Anniversary All-Time Team
  • New England Patriots All-2000s Team
  • New England Patriots All-2010s Team

The Greatest Comeback In Sports History:

This comeback made me appreciate Tom Brady's Greatness after years of hating him.

Falcons Vs Patriots in 2017, Tom Brady Carried His Team From a 28-3 Deficit. No one has ever done that in football, which made me respect and appreciate his determination.
